ABOUT

TeensLight Africa is an initiative which aims to support and guide teenagers in Africa to become well-rounded individuals with a clear sense of purpose and values


Our goal is to connect with teen in our local community and beyound, through various media channels, to help them explore their interest and passion. By doing so, we hope to inspire them to live a life that is true to who they are and what they want to achieve.


We believe that when young people have a sense of purpose, they are more likely to make positive choices and contributions to their communities. And that's exact;y what we want to see - a generation of teenagers who are motivated, confident, and determined to make a difference
