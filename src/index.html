Meet Our Pionner

Miss Miracle Osas Maxwell is a Seasoned Writer and a teens coach committed to helping young teenagers and adults align with the purpose of their creation. Her ever inspiring teachings and exhortations have being of major Impact in shaping lives in all ramifications including academics, character, purity and self development.

" Growing up as a teenager, I made a lot of mistakes which included wrong choices in friends, wrong use of the internet, and many other. Every wrong choice I made impacted me negatively with harsh repercussions and now that i am grown i wish i never made those mistakes. I cannot change what has happened to me, but i can help the younger generation not to become a victim of such. How do i achieve that by teaching and mentoring them with love. Making them realize why they should dare to stand out, resist pressure, and how to make the right decisions" -Miss Miracle Osas Maxwell

Miss Miracle is also a Seasoned Writer, an orator, a relationship guide and the Pioneer of TEENSLIGHT AFRICA
